03

Cool City

Self-initiated Project

Project Description

Cool City is a self-initiated Web Application designed for urban planners worldwide to analyze heat islands and develop cooling strategies. By integrating AI and GIS analysis, the app processes multiple urban data layers, including temperature, vegetation health (NDVI), land use, traffic, land surface materials, building density & heights, energy consumption, and vulnerability zones; to provide:

  1. Urban heat island mapping
  2. Heat severity scoring
  3. Factor-based heat analysis
  4. AI-driven cooling solutions based on local regulations

This project demonstrates my expertise in both Urban Design and UI/UX Design, bridging data analysis with user-friendly interfaces to help professionals make informed climate adaptation decisions.
